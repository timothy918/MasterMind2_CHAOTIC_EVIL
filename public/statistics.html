Welcome to MasterMind 2: Chaotic Evil

Whether you appreciate the challenging gameplay of MasterMind, the inventive spirit of Not Tetris, or the addictive difficulty of Flappy Bird, you're sure to find something to love in this game. MasterMind, originally introduced by Mordecai MEIROWITZ in 1970, became a classic puzzle game. However, after beating it countless times, I found the challenge to wane. Many mobile games have attempted to ramp up the difficulty by increasing the number of slots or colors, but this often turns the gameplay into a tedious experience. My approach in designing this game was to introduce a bit of chaos, compelling players to hone their judgment amidst uncertainty. THE MAKING-OF

This project, crafted in JavaScript and hosted on Firebase, offers a retro gaming experience. It features a real-time no-SQL database that meticulously records game data, allowing for seamless interactions with user queries through a dynamic pictogram chart created with Chart.js. Clicking on any columns in pictogram chart will visualize a Scatter Plot for more details. You can view the schema of the database, if interested. The game also tracks users' personal best records, provided they accept cookies. Developed under a trial-and-error software development model, this project showcases my dedication to creating engaging and functional gaming experiences. Feel free to check out my personal website for other work of mine. Also, I made a video tour (coming soon) for this web app.
