Welcome to MasterMind 2: Chaotic Evil

Whether you appreciate the challenging gameplay of MasterMind, the inventive spirit of Not Tetris, or the addictive difficulty of Flappy Bird, you're sure to find something to love in this game. MasterMind, originally introduced by Mordecai MEIROWITZ in 1970, became a classic puzzle game. However, after beating it countless times, I found the challenge to wane. Many mobile games have attempted to ramp up the difficulty by increasing the number of slots or colors, but this often turns the gameplay into a tedious experience. My approach in designing this game was to introduce a bit of chaos, compelling players to hone their judgment amidst uncertainty.
