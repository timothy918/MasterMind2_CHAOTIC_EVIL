Instruction

Classic mastermind:-

There are 4 slots and 6 possible colours for each slot. Your goal is to guess the correct combination within a limited number of chances. Every time you make a guess, the machine will provide hints based on your guess:
Ⓡ (for rights) repersents a correct colour in the correct slot;
Ⓦ (for wrongs) repersents a correct colour in a wrong slot.

If you complete all levels in the game mode you choose, the result will be ranked by number of chance(s) remaining then by time taken per level.
