Instruction

Classic mastermind:

There are 4 slots and 6 possible colors for each slot. Your goal is to guess the correct combination within a limited number of chances. Every time you make a guess, the machine will provide hints based on your guess:
Ⓡ (for rights) repersents a correct color in the correct slot;
Ⓦ (for wrongs) repersents a correct colour in a wrong slot.

If you complete all levels in the game mode you choose, the result will be ranked by number of chance(s) remaining then by time taken per level.

FAQs:

Why is the machine giving hints other than Ⓡ/Ⓦ?

It's okay. After you get the correct combination the hints for rights will light up green.


Why is the machine is giving different hints every turn?

Just take a guess.


Why are there more than 6 possible colours?

More fun for you.
